How to Find an Exoplanet

Overview

How to Find an Exoplanet | TED-Ed:

In this lesson, you'll learn about:

  • Introduces the different techniques scientists use to find exoplanets, including astrometry, the Doppler effect, and direct imaging.
  • Explains the limitations of current technology and how upcoming missions may improve the search for exoplanets.
  • Highlights some of the most significant exoplanet discoveries and what they tell us about the diversity of planetary systems​​
